AI / Machine Learning / Analytics

NovaStyle AI Marketing Intelligence System

Built an end-to-end AI-powered analytics system that predicts campaign response, identifies underperforming channels, and generates executive recommendations automatically.

Python Scikit-learn OpenAI API Streamlit Pandas
86% ML model accuracy — Random Forest Classifier predicting customer campaign response across 5 channels.
View on GitHub ↗
The Problem
NovaStyle was spending on ads without clarity on what was working. Rising acquisition costs, low campaign acceptance rates, and no reliable view of customer behavior made budget allocation essentially guesswork.
What I Built
  • Analyzed customer behavior across 5 marketing channels and calculated advanced KPIs
  • Trained a Random Forest Classifier (86% accuracy) to predict which customers would respond to campaigns
  • Integrated the OpenAI API to auto-generate 5 executive-level strategic recommendations from the data
  • Packaged everything into a live interactive Streamlit dashboard
Key Findings
  • Email had the highest campaign acceptance rate
  • Instagram significantly underperformed all other channels
  • Customers aged 60+ are the highest-value segment
  • Total spend and recency are the strongest predictors of response
Business Impact
Identified Instagram as a budget drain — recommended reallocation toward Email and TikTok. Predictive model enables targeting based on likelihood to respond, saving wasted spend. AI-generated recommendations give non-technical stakeholders instant strategic direction.

Analytics / GA4

E-Commerce Retention Analysis

Android traffic dropped 31% while revenue grew — revealing a retention risk hidden beneath strong top-line performance.

GA4 Excel Data Analysis
Identified mobile retention weakness masked by revenue growth — recommended fixing engagement before scaling acquisition.
Overview
Strong top-line revenue masked a quieter problem: returning users were declining. The data suggested the business was growing on the surface while losing its most valuable customers underneath.
What I Did
Analyzed acquisition, engagement, and device-level performance in GA4 and Excel. Compared returning-user behavior across platforms and channels to pinpoint where drop-off was happening.
Key Finding
Android traffic had declined ~31% year-over-year, alongside lower engagement time on mobile — a likely mobile experience issue that revenue figures alone would never surface.
Recommendation
Fix mobile retention before pushing acquisition further. Growth built on a leaky mobile experience compounds the problem — it doesn't solve it.
Data Analysis / Tableau

Student Mental Health Trends Analysis

80,000+ records across 4 years — and a finding that challenged the assumption that more support equals better outcomes.

Tableau Excel Longitudinal Analysis
Therapy and medication usage increased post-pandemic, but academic impairment remained high — support access alone wasn't producing recovery.
What I Did
Standardized multi-year datasets in Excel, aligned inconsistent variables, and built Tableau dashboards tracking depression, anxiety, suicidal ideation, therapy usage, medication usage, and academic impairment over time.
Key Findings
Mental health indicators peaked during the pandemic and stayed elevated afterward. Therapy and medication usage increased — but academic impairment remained high in post-pandemic periods.
Recommendations
  • Earlier intervention before crisis points
  • Peer-support models to complement clinical services
  • Academic accommodation frameworks accounting for lingering mental health impact
Why It Mattered
More support isn't always better support. This reframed the conversation from "are we providing enough resources" to "are the right resources reaching students at the right time."
